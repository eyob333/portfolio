About me

Hi, I'm Winter-Nomad — a passionate software engineer and web developer based in Ethiopia. I bring a strong technical foundation in JavaScript, Python, C, C++, GLSL, and Go, along with hands-on experience in building scalable and engaging web applications. My toolbox includes modern technologies like React, Three.js, Vite, and Express, which I use to create both seamless user experiences and robust backend systems.

Throughout my journey, I’ve worked on diverse projects—from responsive websites and real-time 3D visualizations to backend services and system-level applications. I enjoy the challenge of solving complex problems and transforming ideas into well-architected, high-performance solutions.

I'm currently diving deeper into database systems, expanding my knowledge in data modeling, performance optimization, and system architecture. I’m driven by a constant desire to learn, improve, and collaborate with others who share a passion for impactful technology.

When I’m not coding, I’m exploring new ideas, reading about emerging technologies, and contributing to projects that push the boundaries of what’s possible.
